About Attention Detection

Drowsiness (EAR)

Monitors eye closure using Eye Aspect Ratio. When your eyes stay closed for the threshold duration, you'll be alerted.

Microsleep

Detects longer periods of eye closure (3+ seconds by default), indicating brief sleep episodes.

Yawning

Monitors mouth opening patterns to detect yawning, an early sign of fatigue.

Gaze Direction

Tracks where you're looking to detect when your attention drifts from the screen.
